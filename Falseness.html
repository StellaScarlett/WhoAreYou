Falseness

Not according with truth or fact; incorrect.

Made to imitate something in order to deceive.

When we think of falseness online we tend to think of Catfish. A term which became well-known after the TV Show of the same name, hosted by Nev Schulman and Max Joseph, aired in 2012. The act of taking someone elses identity generally used to persue deceptive online romances is more common than you would think but why?

Generally it is out of insecurity but other times it can be as a spite for revenge.
